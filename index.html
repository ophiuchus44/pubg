Rules:
#1 - Treat every game as intended. Try to win.
#2 - If you find that your teammates are playing in the wrong server and have trouble communicating with you. Follow the interigation rules.

Rules for Interrigation:
#1 - Kill them. The best way is to lure them in by getting your team to meet up to exchange ammo.
#2 - Once they are dead, use google translator to interigate them.
#3 - Do not let them die. Keep reviving them during the interigation.

Suggested Questions for Interrigation:
Why do you play on our servers?
Expected Answer:
Their server has too many hackers.
Respond by:
Inviting them to join the PSP and tell them to protect their local communities instead of running away.
or
Kill them and tell them to play on their own servers.

This is a team game that requires constant communication and there is nothing more frustrating than playing a game with people who don't speak your language when you are on your home server. If you want to help, sign up below.
